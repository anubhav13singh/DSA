Given first term (a), common difference (d) and a integer N of the Arithmetic Progression series, the task is to find Nth term of the series. **Example 1:** Input : a = 2 d = 1 N = 5 Output : 6 The 5th term of the series is : 6 **Example 2:** Input : a = 5 d = 2 N = 10 Output : 23 The 10th term of the series is : 23
